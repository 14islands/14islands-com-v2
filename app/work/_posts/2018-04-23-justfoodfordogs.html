{% markdown %} Dogs bring tons of love and joy to their owners and we wanted to show that same personal feeling and playfulness in every step of the experience. At the same time, we wanted to communicate transparency and expertise that play a big part in the quality of JustFoodForDog's products. {% markdown %} ## A personalised meal plan On signup, the service recommends a recipe by leading the customers through some simple questions about their dog. We wanted that flow to be a delightful moment for the owners, where they learn fun facts about their pets and get all relevant informations they want to know about the recipes and their dog‚Äôs needs. More than the recipe, the recommendation engine calculates quantities based on energetic needs, frequency of delivery and estimated delivery dates. {% endmarkdown %}
