A better way for creatives to sell online.

Tictail is a Stockholm-based startup that enables creative people to easily sell online. They wanted a modern website to help their community to succeed.

Crafting it

The site experience is responsive for all devices and features nice transitions to make it pleasant to explore. The focus is on the content and making it easy for people to signup to Tictail.

We also put a lot of effort into performance to keep it fast. The site was built in Wordpress so all content is fully editable by the people at Tictail.

Built responsive from mobile up to big screens
